LightPath Curriculum

Volunteer-friendly children’s ministry curriculum designed to help churches teach the Bible clearly and consistently.

Simple. Biblical. Volunteer-Friendly.

LightPath Curriculum is built for real churches and real volunteers. Every lesson follows a clear structure that helps ministry teams focus on teaching Scripture while keeping preparation simple.

Weekly Lesson Flow
  • Welcome activity
  • Worship or song
  • Bible teaching
  • Discussion
  • Activity or game
  • Prayer

Designed for Churches of Every Size

LightPath Curriculum is designed to support both small and large churches. Our goal is to provide high-quality resources that help churches disciple children clearly while keeping curriculum accessible and easy to implement.
