LightPath Curriculum

Volunteer-friendly children's ministry curriculum designed to help churches teach the Bible clearly and consistently.

Designed for Real Churches

LightPath Curriculum is built specifically for volunteer-led ministries. The goal is simple: provide clear, biblical teaching while making preparation easy for ministry teams.

Weekly Lesson Structure

  • Welcome activity
  • Worship or song
  • Bible teaching
  • Discussion
  • Activity or game
  • Prayer

Accessible for Churches of Every Size

LightPath is designed to serve both growing and established churches. The curriculum is simple to implement, flexible for different group sizes, and grounded in clear biblical teaching.
