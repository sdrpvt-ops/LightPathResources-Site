Our Mission

Children are most open to the Gospel during the ages of 5–12. LightPath exists to help churches reach children during these formative years through simple, biblically grounded curriculum that volunteers can teach with confidence.

LightPath Curriculum is designed specifically for volunteer-led children's ministries. Each lesson follows a clear and repeatable structure that helps churches teach the Bible consistently while making preparation simple for ministry teams.

Weekly Lesson Flow
  • Welcome activity
  • Worship or song
  • Bible teaching
  • Discussion
  • Activity or game
  • Prayer
