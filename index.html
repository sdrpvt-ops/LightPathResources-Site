Our Mission

Children are most open to the Gospel during the ages of 5–12. LightPath exists to help churches reach children during these formative years through simple, biblically grounded curriculum that volunteers can teach with confidence.

LightPath Curriculum

LightPath Curriculum is designed specifically for volunteer-led children's ministries. Each lesson follows a simple and repeatable structure that helps churches teach the Bible clearly and consistently.

Weekly Lesson Flow
  • Welcome activity
  • Worship or song
  • Bible teaching
  • Discussion
  • Activity or game
  • Prayer
